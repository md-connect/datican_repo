Who is DATICAN?

Collaborators

Data Science and Medical Image Analysis for Improved Healthcare Delivery in Nigeria (DATICAN) is a consortium of medical experts and computer scientists from the Lagos State University, University of Ibadan, Redeemer’s University and University of Chicago. The focus of DATICAN is to build capacity in data science and medical image analysis with application in medical image analysis. Goals

DATICAN aims to produce medical Data Scientists who are well equipped to conduct medical research tilted towards improving health care delivery in Nigeria. DATICAN comprises of experts in Data Science, Medical practitioners, Physicists, Mathematicians, Computer scientists and other allied fields. For more information about DATICAN,

Funders

DATICAN is funded by NIH (https://www.nih.gov/) through DSI Africa scheme. We also got additional funding from the center for global health, university of Chicago, USA.
