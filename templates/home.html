Goals

Why DATICAN Repo?

📊

Background

Human health remains a major global concern. Cardiovascular diseases cause about 17.9 million deaths annually, while approximately 1.28 billion adults have hypertension, 422 million live with diabetes, and breast cancer accounts for roughly 685,000 deaths each year worldwide.

⚡

Motivation

Data science has been demonstrated to be useful in early diagnosis of medical conditions in the developed world but Africa is lagging mainly because of:

  • • Lack of usable data
  • • Limited computational resources
  • • Shortage of data scientists in Africa
💡

Solution

DATICAN aims to build capacity in data science for medical applications. It has trained 72 medical and computer scientists and recently acquired HPC resources. This repository provides datasets for researchers applying artificial intelligence in medicine.
