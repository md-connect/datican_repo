About Us

Who is DATICAN?

Collaborators

Data Science and Medical Image Analysis for Improved Healthcare Delivery in Nigeria (DATICAN) is a consortium of medical experts and computer scientists from the Lagos State University, University of Ibadan, Redeemer's University and University of Chicago. The focus of DATICAN is to build capacity in data science and medical image analysis with application in medical image analysis. DATICAN is funded by NIH as part of UE5 grant scheme called DS-I Africa. Four universities are members of DATICAN.

LASU UI RUN UChicago

Goals

DATICAN aims to produce medical Data Scientists who are well equipped to conduct medical research tilted towards improving health care delivery in Nigeria. DATICAN comprises of experts in Data Science, Medical practitioners, Physicists, Mathematicians, Computer scientists and other allied fields.
